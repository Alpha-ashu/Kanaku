## Who Is a Client?

A Client is an End User who chooses to work with a verified financial advisor through the Finora platform. By requesting or booking an advisor session, you automatically accept these Client Terms.

You become a Client when:
- You send a booking request to an advisor
- An advisor accepts your request

You remain an End User for all other features at all times.

---

## What You Can Do as a Client (Additional Features)

### Advisor Discovery
- Browse verified, approved advisor profiles
- View advisor specializations, experience, fees, and ratings
- Filter advisors by expertise (budgeting, investments, tax, debt, etc.)
- View advisor availability calendar

### Booking & Scheduling
- Request a session with any available advisor
- Choose session type: one-time consultation or recurring package
- Select date, time, and session duration
- Receive booking confirmation and reminders via in-app notification and email

### Session Access
- Join live sessions via Finora's in-app messaging and chat interface
- Share specific financial data snapshots with your advisor (you control exactly what is shared)
- View session notes and recommendations your advisor provides after the session
- Access your full session history at any time

### Controlled Data Sharing
- You decide which categories of your data an advisor can see
- Options: transactions only / budgets + goals / full dashboard / nothing (chat only)
- You can revoke an advisor's access to your data at any time from Settings
- Advisors cannot modify your data — they can only view what you explicitly share

### Payments
- Pay for sessions through Finora's secure payment gateway
- View payment history and receipts for all advisor fees
- Choose pay-per-session or subscribe to a package

### Reviews & Ratings
- Rate your advisor after each session (1–5 stars)
- Leave a written review visible to other clients
- Report unprofessional conduct through the dispute system

## Your Responsibilities as a Client (Dos)

| Do | Why |
|----|-----|
| Provide accurate financial information when sharing with your advisor | Ensures you receive useful, relevant guidance |
| Show up on time for scheduled sessions | Advisors have limited availability; late cancellations affect their schedule |
| Cancel at least 24 hours in advance if unable to attend | Gives the advisor time to schedule another client |
| Review advisor credentials in their profile before booking | Ensures you choose the right expertise for your needs |
| Use the in-app dispute system if you have a concern | Ensures fair resolution with a proper record |
| Treat your advisor with respect in all communications | Required by our platform conduct standards |
| Keep your payment method up to date | Prevents failed payments and session cancellation |

## What Your Advisor Can and Cannot Do With Your Data

### Your Advisor CAN:
- View the financial data categories you explicitly approve
- Read your transaction history (if you share it)
- Leave session notes and recommendations in your account
- See your goals and budgets (if you share them)
- Message you through Finora's in-app chat

### Your Advisor CANNOT:
- Add, edit, or delete any of your transactions or records
- See data categories you have not shared
- Access your account without an active, confirmed booking
- Contact you outside Finora's platform
- Share your data with third parties
- See your passwords, PIN, or payment information

## Session Cancellation & Refunds

| Situation | Policy |
|-----------|--------|
| Client cancels 24+ hours before session | Full refund |
| Client cancels less than 24 hours before | No refund (credit to next session at advisor's discretion) |
| Advisor cancels | Full refund + ability to rebook at priority |
| Technical issue prevents session | Full refund or free rescheduling |
| No-show (client) | No refund |
| No-show (advisor) | Full refund + formal complaint filed against advisor |

## Dispute Resolution

If you have a complaint about an advisor:
1. First, raise it in the session chat and try to resolve directly.
2. If unresolved, use **Settings > My Sessions > [Session] > Report Issue**.
3. Finora's Trust & Safety team will review within 5 business days.
4. For refund disputes: escalate via Settings > Payments > Dispute.

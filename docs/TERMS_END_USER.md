## Who Is an End User?

An End User is anyone who creates a Kanaku account to manage their **personal finances**. This includes tracking income and expenses, setting budgets, monitoring goals, scanning receipts, tracking investments, and managing loans and bills.

You do **not** need to book advisor sessions to be an End User. The End User role is the foundation of all other roles — Clients and Advisors start as End Users.

---

## What You Can Do (Your Features)

### Expense & Income Tracking
- Create, edit, and delete transactions manually
- Import bank statements (CSV, PDF, Excel formats)
- Categorize transactions automatically or manually
- Add notes, tags, and attachments to any transaction

### Budget Management
- Set monthly or custom-period budgets per category
- Receive alerts when approaching or exceeding budget limits
- View budget vs. actual spending summaries

### Financial Goals
- Create savings goals with target amounts and deadlines
- Track progress and receive milestone notifications
- Link goals to specific accounts or categories

### Receipt Scanning (AI-Powered)
- Take a photo or upload a receipt image
- AI automatically extracts merchant, date, amount, and line items
- Review and confirm extracted data before it is saved
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, PDF

### Voice Logging
- Speak a transaction (e.g., "Spent 500 rupees on groceries") to log it instantly
- Voice is processed locally and not stored after the transaction is created
- Supports natural language in multiple languages

### Bills & Recurring Transactions
- Add recurring bills with due dates
- Set up automatic transaction creation for recurring income/expenses
- Receive reminders before bill due dates

### Investment Tracking
- Add stocks, mutual funds, gold, and other assets
- Track portfolio value and performance over time
- View gain/loss summaries

### Loans & Borrowing
- Track loans you have taken or given
- Log repayments and view outstanding balances

### Group Expenses
- Split expenses with friends or family
- Track who owes whom and settle debts
- Create expense groups for trips, shared households, etc.

### Tax Tracking
- Categorize transactions as tax-deductible
- Generate a tax summary report for the financial year

### Data Export
- Export all transactions, budgets, and reports as CSV or PDF
- Download full data backup (JSON) from Settings

### Offline Mode
- All core features work without an internet connection
- Data syncs automatically when connection is restored

## Your Data as an End User

**What we collect:**
- Financial transactions you enter or import
- Receipt images you upload (stored securely in Kanaku cloud storage)
- Budget and goal settings
- Device information (for sync purposes)
- App usage analytics (anonymized)

**What we do not collect:**
- Your bank account login credentials (we use file import, not screen scraping)
- Raw voice recordings (processed and discarded immediately)
- Biometric data

**Your data rights:**
- **View:** All your data is visible in the app
- **Edit:** You can edit any data you created
- **Delete:** Delete individual items or your entire account
- **Export:** Download your full data at any time
- **Portability:** Export to standard formats (CSV, JSON)
